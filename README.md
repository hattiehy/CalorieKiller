# CalorieKiller
An Android assistant for children weight management.
Obesity and weight management for  primary school aged children (5-12 years) is a significant problem within Australia, with 1 in 4 children being either overweight or obese. These children who are overweight or obese face an increased risk of both mental and physical health problems.
